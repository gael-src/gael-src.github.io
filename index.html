About

Hello World!

I am Gaël, web developer in education. Nice to e-meet you.

One year ago, I decided to follow my passion and start a new Career as a web developer. I enrolled in the DCI Berlin Full Stack Web development course in September 2019, also I was hired as a student tutor until December 2020.

The DCI offers an intensive hands-on one year web development course based on the MERN Stack. The course focussed on topics like responsive web development with CSS and React, Linux, MongoDB, HTML5, ECMAScript 6, NodeJS and other web related technologies. I have also been a happy Ubuntu user for the past 5 years.

As the Tutor and Speaker of my class I proved that I am team-oriented and passionate about helping others learn new concepts. I am a self-motivated learner and spend much of my free time exploring and expanding on topics I learned in class. Additionally, from my years working as a manager in the gastronomy and service sectors, I work effectively and seamlessly in teams in fast-paced and stressful environments.

I am open to work, feel free to contact me for more information.
